My Personal Website for DART 449

Midterm

Using HTML5 and CSS, this website take after my personal website's aesthetics and represents my midterm proposal for project 2, which is consisted of 3 pages. The first page, which is the landing page, is the proposal essay discussing my chosen research topic discussing the environmental issues. This essay is adapted to the website under the format of body text but it is an equivalent to a 3-page essay. That said, the second page shows a style guide that icludes what I plan to use to build my website. It is consisted of colours, typogrpahy and images of inspirational websites. Last but not least, the third page showcases the mockups of my website consisting of 4 screenshots for my desktop UI design, a 1 mobile design and a description of how the interactivity will take place.

My Proposal on Endangered Sea Turtles

Final Project

Using HTML5 and CSS, this website discusses 4 endangered species of sea turtles under a narrative format, meaning as a story.Each tale about an endangered turtles talks about how one can help these turtles live a betetr and happier life. To do that, we must spread awareness and do beyond recycling and be sustainable to get rid of climate changes, so these innocent creatures can be saved.
